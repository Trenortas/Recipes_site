Pepperoni

Description

Peperoni (italian peperoni) is a spicy variety of salami of Italian-American origin, made from cured meat and flavored with paprika or varieties of chili pepper, and also the name of a pizza of American origin.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough - 1 piece
  • Mozzarella cheese - 250 g.
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Smoked sausage - 200 g.
  • Chilli pepper - 1 piece
  • Tomatoes in their own juice - 1 jar
  • Oregano - 1 teaspoon
  • Dried basil - 1 teaspoon
  • Garlic - 1 clove
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Sali - on taste
  • Ground black pepper - on taste

Cooking steps

  1. Pizza sauce: mix tomatoes with spices, chopped garlic, sugar, salt and pepper.
  2. Boil.
  3. Roll out the dough, brush it with olive oil and sauce.
  4. Sprinkle with half the cheese, add sausage and thinly sliced chili.
  5. Top with remaining cheese.
  6. Cook in the oven for 10-12 minutes at 220 degrees.
